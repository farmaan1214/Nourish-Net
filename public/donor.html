💡 Donation Tips

  • Cooked food should be donated within 2 hours of preparation
  • Package food securely in covered containers
  • Label items with allergen information when possible
  • Provide accurate expiry times

📜 Guidelines

We accept cooked meals, fresh produce, packaged foods, and beverages. We do not accept alcohol, expired foods, or items with damaged packaging.
