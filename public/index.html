About NourishNet
Fighting Hunger.
Eliminating Waste.
to feed hunger and to prevent food wastage with an ai integrated system developed by farmaan of final year student studying b.tech aids.
🍽

Feed the Hungry

We connect restaurants, events, and individuals with NGOs and shelters to ensure no cooked meal goes to waste while people go hungry.

♻️

Prevent Food Waste

Over 40% of food produced globally is wasted. NourishNet creates a real-time bridge to redirect surplus before it expires.

🤖

AI-Powered Matching

Our intelligent system predicts demand, optimises pickup routes, and matches donors to receivers based on location, quantity and urgency.

🌐

Community Network

Every meal saved strengthens a community. We build trust between donors and NGOs through verified profiles and transparent tracking.

AI Integration

NourishNet leverages machine learning for demand forecasting, natural language search for food matching, smart expiry alerts, and predictive analytics to maximise every donation's impact — ensuring food reaches the right hands at the right time.
